Starline TapBox Rental

These are used to provide a connection system for overhead power distribution networks, so that a load bank and cable can be used to commissioning the busway and verify operation.

These are only compatible with the B400T5 series busway, and rated for 600V 400A.

Rental Rates: $150 Day/Week $450 Month

Weight: 40lbs. Dimensions: 8x12x20 inches (LxWxH)

Tap-off boxes are used to draw power from the busway and have a special plug head (paddle) which inserts into the busway track slot. Twist the tap-off box 90 degrees to make electrical connection. The blades in the paddle assembly fit into the busbar channel inside the busway track for a compression connection.

  • Save time comissioning your datacenter, without the need to run cables from your main power buss to load banks.
  • Built-in female CamLoc connectors for quick connection of cables.
  • Ground and neutral connections allow for WYE configured loads and unbalanced load tests.

For maximum safety follow NFPA 70e guidelines for proper installation and de-installation of tap-off boxes from busway power distribution systems.
